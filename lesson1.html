Lesson 1 of 4

White Australia Policy & The Stolen Generations

💭 Thinking Question

Imagine the government passed a law saying only certain people were welcome in Australia and everyone else must leave or change who they are. Who would be affected? How would people react today?

🎯 Learning Intention

By the end of this lesson I can explain the purpose of the White Australia Policy and Assimilation Policy, describe who was affected, and analyse primary sources including infographics to understand the impact of the Stolen Generations.

⚠️ Warning: This lesson contains historical images, stories and film involving First Nations peoples, including images of deceased persons. Please engage with care and respect.
