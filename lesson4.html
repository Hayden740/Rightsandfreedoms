Lesson 2 of 5

Assimilation Policy & The Stolen Generations

💭 Thinking Question

Imagine being taken from your family as a child and told that your culture, your language, and your identity are worthless. How would that affect you — not just then, but for the rest of your life?

🎯 Learning Intention

By the end of this lesson I can explain the Assimilation Policy and why the Australian government created it, describe the impact of the Stolen Generations on First Nations communities, and analyse primary sources including infographics and personal accounts.

⚠️ Warning: This lesson contains historical images, personal accounts and film involving First Nations peoples, including images of deceased persons. Please engage with care and respect.
