Lesson 1 of 5

The White Australia Policy

💭 Thinking Question

Imagine the government passed a law saying only certain people were welcome in Australia and everyone else must leave or change who they are. Who would be affected? How would people react today?

🎯 Learning Intention

By the end of this lesson I can explain the purpose of the White Australia Policy, describe who was affected and how, and analyse primary sources including political quotes and cartoons.

⚠️ Warning: This lesson contains historical images and film involving First Nations peoples. Please engage with care and respect.

The White Australia Policy

Study the information page and historical sources from your booklet:

FederationA group of states joined under one government.
ImmigrationComing to live permanently in a foreign country.
RestrictionA limiting legal measure.
DeportedExpelled from a country on grounds of illegal status.
ProhibitedForbidden; banned by law.
Dictation testWriting a passage aloud — used to exclude non-Europeans.

White Australia Policy Questions

1What was the aim of the White Australia Policy? Was it successful?
2Who suffered because of this policy, and how?
3What was the purpose of the Immigration Restriction Act 1901?
4Why do you think the Government excluded certain groups from immigrating?
5How were people encouraged to immigrate? What groups were targeted?
