Acknowledgement of Country

Seaford Secondary College is built on Kaurna Land. We acknowledge and respect the history and custodianship of the Kaurna people and recognise the important contributions of past and present leaders in building the community.

We also pay our respects to all language groups throughout South Australia and their cultures, as well as to all Aboriginal Elders past and present.

Warning: Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander viewers are warned that the following pages contain film, voices and images of deceased persons.

Disclaimer: This booklet contains historically accurate language used to describe First Nations People that is now deemed inappropriate for modern use. When discussing First Nations Peoples, please use the following terms: Aboriginal · Torres Strait Islander · First Nations Peoples · Indigenous Australian.
